Amon Tobin - Bricolage

Drum & Bass, Nu Jazz

Late 90s electronic compositions with plenty of jazz instrumentation. Excellent use of subtle bass notes. See also "Dark Jovian".

♫ Sample

Andy Stott - Too Many Voices

Techno

A superb release from Stott; I’d rate it far higher than Faith in Strangers and probably on par with Luxury Problems. Pretty diverse techno record with some well placed nods to footwork and the Detroit scene. Highly recommend.

♫ Sample

Anohni - Hopelessness

Techno

Co-produced by Oneohtrix Point Never and Hudson Mohawke with righteous soulful vocals by Anohni. Politically charged ballads in a strangely sexy R&B form. Highly recommend.

♫ Sample

Benjamin Damage - Obsidian

Techno, House

The final record released on the 50 Weapons label, Spacey, dreamy ambient techno interpolated with some heavy hitters.

♫ Sample

Caroline K - Now Wait for Last Year

Minimal Synth. Ambient

Late 80s synth work, drawn out notes, solid range of sound. Kinda spooky, kinda blissful.

♫ Sample

Atmospheric techno. Slow building tracks with lots of distortion/noise elements.

Demdike Stare - Tryptych

Dark Ambient, Found sounds

A collective of three Demdike records. Sample-based album consisting of a variety of world music, archival library records, etc processed through analog machinery. Incredible range of sounds throughout.

♫ Sample

DJ Paypal - Sold Out

Footwork, Juke

This EP is all over the place while maintaining a steady footwork vibe. Absolutely insane drums. Lots of hype cuts.

♫ Sample

Echospace - Liumin

Dub, Field Recordings

Lowkey atmospheric dub record layered with field recordings of various street sounds in Japan. (subway sounds, conversations, etc) A great listen, Highly recommend.

♫ Sample

Ed Harrison - NEOTOKYO OST

Soundtrack, Orchestral, IDM

Soundtrack for PC game NEOTOKYO. Reminiscent of the music in Halo but far superior in my opinion. Pretty solid album independent of the game.

♫ Sample

El Mahdy Jr. - Ghost Tapes

Dub, Field Recordings

Algerian producer mashing together field recordings, radio frequencies, and found tapes. Excellent samples, reminds me of Nicolas Jaar’s sound selections at points.

♫ Sample

Elysia Crampton - American Drift

Experimental

I’ve listened to this record an ungodly amount of times, and I’m still not really sure how to describe it. Worth a listen. The Money Allah features are great. Highly recommend.

♫ Sample

Enigma - MCMXC a.D

New Age

Important new age record. Some old ass trucker told me to check it out after he heard me playing Oneohtrix at work. Has some hilariously 90’s sound effects. Sounds like a hybrid of a guided meditation and a guided workout video. Bizarre but pretty solid on the whole.

♫ Sample

Function & Vatican Shadow - Games Have Rules

Techno

Atmospheric techno cuts with a couple dance-oriented tracks at the end. Great late night album.

♫ Sample

Jega - Variance

IDM

Rough version of this record leaked back in ‘03. Jega went back and remade the entire album, dropping the new version in '09 as a double disc. Nice old school electronic feel, like a more coherent Aphex Twin. Highly recommend.

♫ Sample

Kane Ikin - Modern Pressure

Downtempo, Techno, Industrial

Bleak, chugging, entrancing tracks. An excellent release from this year. Highly recommend.

♫ Sample

Kangding Ray - Cory Arcane

Techno, House

Hard hitting techno beats, remains both consistent and engaging. Really fun listen.

♫ Sample

Klaus Schulze - Picture Music

Progressive Electronic, Ambient

Tons of Moog sounds and space aesthetic. Not a highly regarded album in Schulze’s discography, but I like how stripped down it is.

♫ Sample

Kuedo - Assertion of a Surrounding Presenc

Progressive Electronic

Blasts of insanely well sequenced aggression, throbbing bass notes, etc. A very cinematic release.

♫ Sample

Lakker - Struggle & Emerge

Techno, House, Field Recordings

Concept album regarding Dutch struggles with the North Sea. Lots of deliberate static/feedback bursts throughout the tape, along with a variety of tempos. Plays with a sense of urgency.

♫ Sample

Mark Fell & Gabor Lazar - The Neurobiology of Moral Decision Making

Hardcore Dance

Ten untitled tracks, each building on the previous. Abstract and hard hitting.

♫ Sample

Micachu - Taz and May Vids

Footwork, Noise Pop

Dropped on Demdike Stare’s label DDS, and featuring a remix by them. Seven tracks, all heaters. Noticeable footwork influence in the first few. Highly recommend.

♫ Sample

Mura Masa - Someday Somewhere

Downtempo, Alt R&B

Great lowkey R&B electronic pieces. The production compliments the featured singers perfectly, although some vocal performances definitely outdo the instrumentals in my opinion. Highly recommend.

♫ Sample

Neat Beats - Cosmic Surgery

Glitch Hop, Breakbeat

Absolutely incredible string and piano arrangements complimented with vocal samples reminiscent of those on Ricky Eat Acid’s Three Love Songs, etc.

♫ Sample

Not Waving - Voices

Drone, Experimental

A reshaping of three limited cassette runs into a new soundscape by rearranging the track listing and removing several tracks. One of the more interesting and engaging drone releases in recent memory. Highly recommend.

♫ Sample

Oliver Coates - Upstepping

Experimental, Afrobeat, Classical Cello

Dance-y electronic record with great afrobeat samples. Coates is a cellist, and makes use of this skill throughout the album to create some truly dynamic soundscapes.

♫ Sample

Raime - Quarter Turns Over a Living Line

Industrial, Techno, Breakbeat

The first release on the Blackest Ever Black label (and still one of my favorites). Gloomy soundscapes with dub elements. A cold and oppressive record. Highly recommend.

♫ Sample

Rainer Veil - New Brutalism

Techno, House

Definitely reflects the brutalist style. Sparse beats, ideal for rainy days.

♫ Sample

Shinichi Atobe - Butterfly Effect

Techno, House

Sounds ranging from proto-vaporwave to straight up techno to drone. Really an incredible piece of work from an artist that rarely puts out music. A current top 10. Highly recommend.

♫ Sample

Slava - Raw Solutions

UK Bass, Footwork

Ranges between really good cuts and comically ignorant dance cuts (see track “Girls on Dick”) A pretty fun listen but not really remarkable compared to anything else on this list.

♫ Sample

Vainio & Vigroux - Peau Froide, Léger Soleil

Industrial Techno

Tough, cold beats. Some of the best album art of 2016. What’s not to love?
